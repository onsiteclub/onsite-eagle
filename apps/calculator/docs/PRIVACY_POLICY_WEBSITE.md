# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2025**

**OnSite Club Inc.** ("we", "us", or "our") operates the OnSite Calculator mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** (required)
- **Name** (first and last name)
- **Password** (encrypted, we cannot see it)
- **Birthday** (optional)
- **Gender** (optional)
- **Trade/Specialty** (optional, if you identify as a construction worker)

### 1.2 Voice Data

When you use the voice input feature:
- **Audio is processed in real-time** to convert speech to text
- **Audio is NOT stored** on our servers after processing
- **Transcribed text may be logged** if you consent to help improve our service
- Voice processing is handled by OpenAI's Whisper API

### 1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect:
- Calculations performed (expressions and results)
- App usage patterns (which features you use)
- Device information (OS version, device model)
- Error logs for troubleshooting

### 1.4 Consent Records

We record when you:
- Accept our Terms of Service
- Accept this Privacy Policy
- Opt-in or opt-out of marketing communications
- Consent to voice data collection for service improvement

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide the App's functionality** (calculations, unit conversions)
- **Process voice commands** for hands-free operation
- **Save your calculation history** (if logged in)
- **Improve our services** based on usage patterns
- **Send communications** (only if you opt-in to marketing)
- **Comply with legal obligations**

---

## 3. Information Sharing

We share your information with:

| Third Party | Purpose | Data Shared |
|-------------|---------|-------------|
| **Supabase** | Authentication & database | Email, profile data |
| **OpenAI** | Voice transcription | Audio recordings (not retained by OpenAI) |
| **Vercel** | App hosting | Server logs |

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal information
- Share data with advertisers
- Use data for targeted advertising

---

## 4. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Account data | Until you delete your account |
| Calculation history | 90 days |
| Voice transcriptions | 30 days (if consented) |
| Consent records | 7 years (legal requirement) |
| Error logs | 30 days |

---

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

### 5.1 Access Your Data
Request a copy of all data we have about you.

### 5.2 Correct Your Data
Update your profile information in the app settings.

### 5.3 Delete Your Data
Delete your account and all associated data through the app menu.

### 5.4 Withdraw Consent
Opt-out of marketing or voice data collection at any time.

### 5.5 Data Portability
Request your data in a machine-readable format.

---

## 6. Data Security

We protect your data using:
- **Encryption in transit** (HTTPS/TLS)
- **Encryption at rest** (database encryption)
- **Row-level security** (you can only access your own data)
- **Secure authentication** (Supabase Auth)

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our App is **not intended for children under 13**. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us.
